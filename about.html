Hey! I'm Peijia and I'm a senior finishing up my last year of Cognitive Psychology at the University of British Columbia in Vancouver, Canada.


I got into design after wondering how I could transform my interests in the social sciences and psychology into actionable outcomes (aka not just papers). I see creativity as a way to solve problems and explore ideas!

I'm interested in consumer/social tech, as well as wearables and health technology. I've been lucky enough to have opportunities in tackling these spaces. Previously, product design at Quorum (YC 2019) & LimeSpot. Also, designed a wearable at New Venture Design and UX/UI design at UBC Launchpad.

Currently, I'm doing product design at Pattern (YC S2020 ), working on the future of hourly work. Concurrently, I'm also working as a HCI research assistant at UBC and BC Children's hospital - working on designing a prototype that communicates risks derived from artificial intelligence analysis.
